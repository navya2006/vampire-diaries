THE VAMPIRE DIARIES

Welcome to the town of Mystic Falls, a town filled with thiller and suspense. The town where Vampires can roam freely in the daylight, do beware on a full moon where you might spot a vicious werewolf hunting down its prey. It is the town where the dead can be bought to life and they may even unite in eternity. The town where the witches can be friendly. The town which even allured the immortal orignal family along with the evil hybrid.



The story is based on the one and only Salvatore Brothers, the most dashing 162 years old vampires, who have fallen for the same girl, Elena Gilbert. To know more about this case there's a long history that dates 100 years back.....
